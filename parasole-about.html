About us

A family-run team, rooted in NEPA.

For over 20 years, Parasole Properties has helped families rent and helped owners build peace of mind, one relationship at a time.

Our story

We treat every property like our own.

Parasole Properties is a family-run business based in Northeastern Pennsylvania. We offer full-service property management, tenant placement, and general contracting, backed by deep local knowledge and decades of hands-on experience in the Scranton/Wilkes-Barre market.

At Parasole, relationships matter. Our residents and owners stay with us because we're responsive, transparent, and genuinely invested in their success, and because you can always reach a real person, fastest by text.
