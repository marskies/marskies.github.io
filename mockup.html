Learn To Leap

A learning platform that meets new instructors where they are — and gets out of their way once they’re moving.
[ Hero screenshot ]
Brief

The ask

New aerial instructors were burning out in their first six months — not from teaching, but from the scaffolding around teaching: lesson prep, sequencing, safety docs, and the quiet expectation that they already knew how it all fit together.

Research

What we found

Interviews with twelve instructors across four studios revealed a pattern: the best mentors taught through a shared vocabulary the platforms didn’t support. We designed around that vocabulary instead of around the LMS.

Outcome

What shipped

Setup time dropped roughly 38% in pilot cohorts, and retention in the first six months climbed meaningfully. The platform is now in use across three partner studios with a fourth onboarding this spring.
